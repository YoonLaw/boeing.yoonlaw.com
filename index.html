Announcement Regarding Lawsuit Against Pinnacle Claims Management, Inc. For Labor Code Violations

We have filed a lawsuit against The Boeing Company (“Boeing”) in the Los Angeles County Superior Court, Case No. 19STCV11220.

This is not a lawsuit against you; you are not being sued.

The lawsuit alleges Boeing’s current and former non-union hourly-paid employees were not paid overtime wages for hours worked over 40 hours in a week. In addition, the lawsuit alleges that employees were not fully relieved of duty for meal periods and rest breaks and were not reimbursed for work-related expenses, amongst other claims. The lawsuit seeks money on behalf of certain hourly-paid employees of Boeing.

WE NEED YOUR HELP

We know you received a letter earlier in this case about the disclosure of your contact information. This letter is different because we are now asking for your help. We have written to you because we believe you would like to know more about the case and because we would like to speak with employees (including former employees) about their experiences working for Boeing. The Court has not yet decided whether this case may proceed as a class action and your input is critical to successfully resolve the lawsuit and achieve the maximum recovery for Boeing’s past and present employees.

If you want us to, we can request your time/payroll and personnel records from Boeing, which will help us build a case to get the best result for everyone. To get your records, we need your written authorization.
